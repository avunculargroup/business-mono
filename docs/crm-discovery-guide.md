# CRM & Discovery Guide

Della — your Relationship Manager — keeps the people and companies that matter most within easy reach. She tracks every interaction, spots relationships that need attention, and helps you turn conversations into clients. Here's what she can do.

---

## CRM: Contacts, Pipeline & Relationship Health

**Contacts & Companies**
Add contacts individually or as part of a company. Link them together, tag them freely, and note anything Della should know. She'll keep the rest up to date as calls, emails, and meetings come in.

**Pipeline**
Every contact moves through five stages: *Lead → Warm → Active → Client → Dormant*. Della tracks movement and flags anyone who's been stuck too long or quietly slipping away.

**Bitcoin Literacy**
Each contact carries a literacy level — *Unknown, None, Basic, Intermediate,* or *Advanced* — so you always know where to pitch the conversation.

**Interactions**
Calls, emails, meetings, Zoom sessions, Signal messages, and notes all log here. Recorder auto-proposes updates from transcripts, so the CRM stays accurate without manual effort.

**Relationship Health**
Della reviews recency, commitments, and pipeline trajectory to surface at-risk or stagnating relationships before they go cold.

---

## Discovery Interviews

Discovery interviews are where market understanding begins. Log each conversation with:

- **Trigger event** — what prompted this company to consider a Bitcoin treasury strategy (FASB change, employee request, regulatory update, or other)
- **Stakeholder role** — CFO, CEO, HR, Treasury, PeopleOps
- **Pain points** — captured and tracked over time as they evolve

**Segment Scorecards** score your target markets by *need* (1–5) and *access* (1–5), helping you focus outreach where it will land best.

**Content Validation** closes the loop: when three or more distinct interviews surface the same pain point, any content linked to it is automatically marked as validated — real signal, not assumptions.

---

## Champions

Champions are your internal advocates inside prospect and client companies. Each one carries a role (*Champion, Economic Buyer,* or *Influencer*), a score from 1–5, and a status: *Active, At Risk,* or *Departed*.

Log events as they happen — a promotion, a job change, a departure. Della and Simon will alert you when something significant changes, because a champion who just moved on is a relationship worth protecting.

---

## Community Watchlist

Track the LinkedIn groups, professional associations, and conferences where your buyers spend time. Mark your engagement as you go: *Not Joined → Joined → Attended → Sponsor*. Rate each community's activity level and tag it by role and industry so you know exactly which ones deserve your energy.
